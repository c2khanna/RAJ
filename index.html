An additional gear has been strategically incorporated in the RAJDHANI gearbox. Thus, it is able to distribute and absorb the 'jerk load' significantly, especially when starting. The result is less impact on the machine, fewer breakdown, smoother running even at heavy loads, and also less wear & tear of components. The gears themselves are manufactured from handpicked and tested alloy steel, which is further toughend with induction and oil quenching procedures.

Filter Press and Oil feed pump

A filter press is a highly efficient, compact, dewatering device for separating solids from liquids slurries in the form of compressed cake. RAJDHANI Brand filter presses are most efficient for collections of purified liquids.
Plate and frame Type Filter Press:
This type of filter press consists of 2 plates and 1 frame to frame to form one unit. The liquid to be filtered is pumped into the press through the side feed holes in the frame passes through the filter cloth to the filter plates and gets out of the pet cock(taps) fitted at the bottom of the filter plate. The precipitate gets collected in the frames in large quantity. Rajdhani filter presses consist of skeleton of two end supports connect by two horizontal parallel supporting plates which supports to plates and frames.

Tightening Arrangement :
Hand Operated tightening arrangement with Screw of gear and ratchet system for tightening (as per the need) is provided to tighten the presses to get maximum filtration capacity and continuous process. To minimize the moisture in the cake (precipitate) steam of dry air can be blown in through the pressure vessel fitted with pressure Gauge fitted at the first feed hole. The working pressure of all the filter presses is upto 5 Kg square cm. whereas all the presses are checked and tested upto the pressure of 100 pounds.
